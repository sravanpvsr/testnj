General Instructions

  • Follow SMS- Sanitization, Mask and Social distancing
  • Use double Mask if you are using a cloth mask especially  in crowded area.
  • Do not bring patients with cold/cough/ fever Or if patient’s family member is suffering from COVID  symptoms/ Covid 19, please postpone your visit to clinic by 2 weeks
  • Please bring spectacle if you are wearing them
  • Please bring your old prescriptions/ eye drops  used/using presently.
  • If vision is less, eye drops are put. This might cause blurring of vision. In adults it might last for a day and blurring of vision will affect driving during those few hours. Where as in children it might be there for 1-2 day period and reading might be difficult for those 1-2 days. So schedule your work accordingly.
